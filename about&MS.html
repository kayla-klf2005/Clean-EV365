Mission Statement

- We at Clean Ev365 believe that it's not too late to make sure there is a cleaner, healthier Trinidad and Tobago (T&T) for future generations to live in and for the present to enjoy. We make it our mission to instill the value of cleaning up after yourself and not disposing of waste in natural environments,to ensure a cleaner T&T, by disposing waste properly, one object at a time.

About

- Hi everyone! My name is Kayla La Fon and I am from Trinidad and Tobago. I am currently in high school. I have had a deep passion for coding and reading books for quite awhile. I chose to create this service so that people, (especially the Citizens of Trinidad and Tobago), can learn about the negative impact of Improper Waste Disposal and instead learn and practice Proper Waste Disposal to prevent these situations in the future.
